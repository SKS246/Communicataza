All About COMMUNI CATAZA :

We are a year old organisation which focuses on helping people around us to build their
communication, confidence, leaderhsip and public speaking skills. With Coommunicataza you
can learn how to communicate, build confidence to move forward in your career and learn
proper ways to give job and college interviews and more.

Join COMMUNI CATAZA And You Will:

Build Leadership Skills
Improve your public speaking skills
Build self-confidence and self-awareness
Work on networking in a small and supportive environment
Practice writing speeches and presenting in a group setting
